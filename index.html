How We Help

User Verification (eKYC) ▶

AML Screening ▶

Know Your Customer (KYC) is the process of verifying the identity of your customers. In Thailand, businesses regulated under AMLO, the Bank of Thailand, or the SEC are required to perform KYC. Even non-regulated businesses benefit from KYC to prevent fraud, reduce chargebacks, and build user trust.
